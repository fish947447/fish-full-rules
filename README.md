I. General Conduct & Etiquette

Respect all members: Treat others with the same level of respect you expect to receive.

No Hate Speech: Harassment, racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination is strictly prohibited.

Keep it Civil: Heated debates are fine; personal attacks and "flaming" are not.

Language: Keep the main chat in English (or your server's primary language) for moderation purposes.

No Drama: Do not bring outside "internet beef" or personal vendettas into public channels.

No Impersonation: Do not pretend to be staff, celebrities, or other members.

No Trolling: Intentionally baiting people into arguments or disrupting the peace will lead to a kick.

Appropriate Nicknames: Keep your display name clean and mentionable (no invisible or offensive characters).

No Ghost Pinging: Do not ping someone and then delete the message immediately.

Listen to Staff: If a Moderator asks you to change the subject or stop a behavior, comply immediately.

II. Messaging & Spam Control

No Spamming: This includes walls of text, repetitive emojis, or "copypastas."

Don’t Overuse Caps: Constant shouting is unnecessary and hard to read.

Use the Correct Channels: Check the channel description before posting (e.g., keep memes in #memes).

No Excessive Pinging: Do not ping @everyone or @here without a very valid reason or permission.

No Spoilers: Use the ||spoiler|| tag for new movies, games, or shows.

No Begging: Do not ask for Nitro, roles, or free items/games.

No Chain Letters: "Post this in 5 servers or you'll have bad luck" messages are banned.

Keep Bot Usage to Bot Channels: Use bot commands (like music or games) only in designated areas.

No Mass Reactions: Do not clutter messages with 20+ different emoji reactions.

Format Code Blocks: If sharing technical snippets or scripts, use proper Markdown formatting.

III. Content Standards (Safety first)

No NSFW Content: This server is SFW. No pornographic, excessively violent, or suggestive material.

No Illegal Activity: Discussing or promoting piracy, hacking, or illegal substances is forbidden.

Privacy: Do not share "dox" or private information (IPs, addresses, real names) of anyone.

Sensitive Topics: Avoid deep discussions on politics or religion unless in a designated "serious" channel.

No Seizure-Inducing Content: Do not post flashing GIFs or videos.

No Malware: Do not post links to suspicious websites or executable files (.exe, .dmg).

Direct Messaging (DM): Do not DM members with advertisements or harassment.

Profile Content: Your Discord status and profile picture must also follow the SFW rules.

No Screamer Links: Do not post links that lead to jump-scares or loud noises.

Respect Copyright: Do not share leaked content or "crack" files for software.

IV. Promotion & Networking

No Self-Promotion: Do not post your Twitch, YouTube, or other Discord links without permission.

No DM Advertising: Unsolicited advertising via DM to members will result in an instant ban.

Partnerships: All partnership requests must go through the Server Owner.

No Scams: Do not post "Get Nitro Free" or "Crypto" links.

Contextual Sharing: If you made something cool, share it in the #creative channel only.

V. Voice Channel (VC) Rules

No Ear-Rape: No screaming, blowing into the mic, or using loud soundboards.

Background Noise: Please use "Push-to-Talk" if you have a noisy environment.

No Channel Hopping: Do not jump rapidly between voice channels.

Recording: Do not record VC audio without the explicit consent of everyone in the room.

Stream Etiquette: If someone is "Go Live," do not join just to disrupt their stream.

VI. Administrative & Legal

Discord ToS: All members must follow the Discord Terms of Service.https://discord.com/terms

Common Sense: If a rule isn't written here, use your best judgment.

Appeals: If you feel a moderation action was unfair, contact a Moderator via DM (if allowed).

No Loophole Seeking: Do not try to find "technicalities" to bypass the spirit of these rules.

Reporting: Use the #report channel or DM staff to report rule-breakers; don't mini-mod.

Right to Update: Staff reserves the right to change these rules at any time with an @announcement.
